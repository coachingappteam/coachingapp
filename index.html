App Features.

A highlight of what our application best offers.

Insightful Analytics

We offer various ways to view the results of your athletes for easier analysis of your training plans.

Performance Analysis

We support an advance machine learning model to determine if your athlete has a high performance in comparison to other athletes in a given training session or meet.

User Friendly

Our application is made with Flutter which offers a flexible and simplistic UI for pleasant experiences.

Collaborative Effort

We enabled the ability of having multiple coaches manage teams and training plans.

Protected Data

We protect your data by keeping it protected and secured in the cloud.

Fast Attendance

The most important of shaping an athlete attending training. We offer easy access for keeping attendance of each athlete.

Athlete Focus

An athlete trains for a specific competition, we offer the ability of assigning roles to athletes that help the machine learning model give an accurate performance analysis.

Membership Tiers

We offer a lot of features and we want to make sure your recieve a service acordingly. Join our membership service to be able to manage more team, athletes and training plans, and even create your own sports, roles and exercises.
